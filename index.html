Lassagna

This is a lassagna recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish composed of layers of wide, flat pasta, meat sauce, and a blend of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses. The pasta is typically layered with the meat sauce and cheese mixture and then baked until the top is golden and bubbly. It's a hearty and comforting dish that is perfect for gatherings and family meals.
